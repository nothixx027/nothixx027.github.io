first & last

A social platform for entrepreneurs to network, collaborate, and make the connections that matter

First & Last was born from a simple observation: every entrepreneur remembers their first connection and their last breakthrough. The platform bridges those two moments — creating a space where founders at every stage can find their people, share their journey, and build partnerships that outlast any single project.
