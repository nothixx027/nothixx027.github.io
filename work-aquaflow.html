aquaflow

A bottled water delivery service that sources water from the purest mountain streams

.aquaflow

Aquaflow began with a simple ambition: to deliver water that tastes the way nature intended. Sourced from the purest mountain streams, the brand challenges the assumption that bottled water must be sterile and impersonal. The visual identity reflects calm, craft, and origin — built around a single typographic mark and a flexible system.

We built the identity around clarity. A single deep blue used at scale, a grid system that adapts across bottle sizes, delivery vans, and the booking app. No mountain photography clichés — just type, color, and restraint.

The result is a brand that feels confident without shouting. Aquaflow now stocks 12 retail partners across two cities and became the second most-stocked still water in independent cafés in its launch market within four months.
