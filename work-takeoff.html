takeoff

A meditation app that uses AI to customise your mindfulness practice and track your progress

Takeoff is a lightweight meditation app that adapts session length, voice, and theme to your habits — rather than forcing you into a fixed routine. The visual system uses warm yellow as a grounding color, with quiet typography that gets out of the way.

We designed Takeoff around three principles: low pressure, high signal, and small surface area. No streaks. No leaderboards. Just sessions that get gradually better at meeting you where you are.
