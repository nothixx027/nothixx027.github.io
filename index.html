The Process

How I Work.

01

Discover & Define

Every project starts with listening. I dig into your brand, audience, and goals before a single pixel is touched. No guesswork — only informed decisions.

02

Concept & Explore

Ideas are sketched, challenged, and filtered. I present focused directions — not a pile of options — so we spend energy on what actually matters.

03

Craft & Refine

The chosen direction is built to production quality. Typography, colour, motion, and every detail are tuned until nothing feels out of place.

04

Deliver & Support

Handoff is thorough — files, guidelines, and a walkthrough so your team can confidently use the work long after the project ends.

Hey, I'm Bharat Arora — a graphic designer and creative based in Delhi, India, specialising in brand identity, UI/UX, and digital experiences that feel as good as they look.

I believe design should communicate before it impresses. Every project starts with deep thinking, moves through careful craft, and ends with something that actually works.

With 2+ years of experience working across startups and independent projects, I've built visual systems that help brands find their voice. Currently open to select freelance projects and creative collaborations.
